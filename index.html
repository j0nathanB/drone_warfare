Drone Warfare

This map shows the locations and human toll of drone strikes in Pakistan, Yemen, Somalia, and Afghanistan from 2004 to 2020. Data was provided by the Bureau of Investigative Journalism's Drone Warfare project (now Airwars).

The map is interactive. Click on each shaded region to see a breakdown.
