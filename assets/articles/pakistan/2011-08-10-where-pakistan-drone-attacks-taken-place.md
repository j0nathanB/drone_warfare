title: Where have Pakistan drone attacks taken place?
date: 2011-08-10
authors: David Pegg
content: |
  The Bureau of Investigative Journalism created an interactive geographic database documenting drone strikes in Pakistan using Tableau visualization software.

  The database includes available details such as "the date, time, region, target or target organisation, number of deaths, number of civilian deaths, number of child deaths, number of injured" along with circumstantial summaries for each incident.

  The article explains that users can search by multiple parameters including Bureau ID, location, date, and target. It notes that larger icons represent strikes with higher casualty counts. The tool also includes a timeline slider allowing viewers to observe strike patterns build up geographically over time.

  The piece emphasizes this represents an "ongoing project" by the organization investigating CIA drone operations in Pakistan, and invites readers to subscribe to their newsletter for updates on the issue.
